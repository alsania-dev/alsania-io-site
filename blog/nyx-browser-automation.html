Nyx: Browser Automation for AI Platforms

March 20, 2025 | Sigma | 📁 Tools & Automation

You're talking to an AI. It suggests running a command, reading a file, or searching the web. Wouldn't it be easier if you could just click a button?

That's what Nyx does. A browser extension that integrates MCP tools directly with your favorite AI platforms.

DeepSeek Gemini Perplexity Grok ChatGPT GitHub Copilot Mistral Kimi Qwen OpenRouter Google AI Studio T3 Chat Z Chat

13+ platforms. One extension.

How It Works

When an AI responds with a tool call, Nyx detects it and adds an execution button. One click, and the tool runs — filesystem operations, terminal commands, API calls, anything the MCP server supports.

The results feed back into your conversation. The AI sees them. The workflow continues.

Why It Matters

Most AI platforms are chat-only. You ask, they answer. That's fine for questions, but useless for action. Nyx bridges that gap. It turns AI from a conversational partner into an operational one.

And it's free. Open source. MIT licensed.

Get It

Nyx is available as a browser extension for Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Download from alsania.io/tools/nyx or the Firefox Addon store.
