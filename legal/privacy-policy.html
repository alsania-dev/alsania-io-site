Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Alsania ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and with our products. This Privacy Policy explains our online information practices and the choices you can make about how your information is used.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Email address (when you join our waitlist or contact us)
  • Name and contact information (via contact forms)
  • Any other information you voluntarily submit

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Page interactions and behavior via Google Analytics
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (for security and analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve our website and services
  • To send you updates about Alsania projects and products
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sovereignty & Security

Alsania believes in data sovereignty. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is treated with the highest level of respect and security. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, and destruction of your information.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Analytics to track visitor behavior and improve our services. Google has its own privacy policy, and you can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

When you visit our shop on Dashery or Redbubble, those platforms have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their data practices.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Google Analytics uses cookies to track usage. You can disable these through your browser or use the Google Analytics Opt-out tool.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

For users in the EU, Alsania complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For users in California, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You have rights regarding your personal information as outlined by these regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of our Privacy Policy.
