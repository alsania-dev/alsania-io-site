Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Alsania is committed to customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be issued for purchases made through our website or affiliated platforms.

2. Dashery Store Refunds

Official Alsania Merchandise (Dashery)

Alsania merchandise sold through Dashery is subject to Dashery's refund and return policies. Please review Dashery's terms at https://dashery.com for specific details regarding:

  • Return windows and deadlines
  • Condition requirements for returns
  • Return shipping procedures
  • Refund processing timelines

For issues with your Dashery order, contact Dashery directly at their support channels.

3. Redbubble Store Refunds

Community Designs (Redbubble)

Community merchandise sold through Redbubble is subject to Redbubble's refund and return policies. Please review Redbubble's terms at https://www.redbubble.com for specific details regarding:

  • Return windows and deadlines
  • Condition requirements for returns
  • Return shipping procedures
  • Refund processing timelines

For issues with your Redbubble order, contact Redbubble directly at their support channels.

4. Digital Products

Digital products (software, extensions, tools) are non-refundable once downloaded or activated, as they cannot be returned or resold. However, if you experience technical issues or the product does not function as advertised, please contact us for support or potential remediation.

5. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive merchandise that is defective, damaged, or significantly different from the product description:

  • Contact the platform (Dashery or Redbubble) immediately
  • Provide photos documenting the issue
  • Follow the platform's dispute or return process

Most platforms offer replacement or refund for genuinely defective items.

6. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation policies depend on your order status:

  • Before fulfillment: Contact the platform immediately for cancellation
  • After fulfillment: Subject to the respective platform's return policy
  • Digital products: Generally cannot be cancelled once activated

7. Third-Party Responsibility

Alsania does not directly process refunds for merchandise purchased through Dashery or Redbubble. These third-party platforms are responsible for handling returns, refunds, and customer disputes. Alsania is not liable for third-party refund policies or processing delays.

8. Software and Tool Refunds

Software products and browser extensions provided by Alsania are provided "as is." Refunds are generally not available, but if you report genuine bugs or functionality issues, we will:

  • Investigate the issue
  • Provide updates or patches
  • Offer technical support
  • Consider refunds on a case-by-case basis for significant technical failures

9. Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are typically the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is defective or was shipped incorrectly. Some platforms offer prepaid return labels for qualifying returns.

10. Refund Processing Time

Refund processing times vary by platform and payment method:

  • Dashery: 5-10 business days after return receipt and approval
  • Redbubble: 5-10 business days after return receipt and approval
  • Credit/debit cards: Additional 1-3 business days for credit processing

11. Contact for Refund Inquiries

For merchandise purchased through our affiliated platforms:

  • Dashery purchases: Contact Dashery support directly
  • Redbubble purchases: Contact Redbubble support directly
  • Alsania-related issues: Contact Alsania at hello@alsania.io

12. Policy Changes

Alsania reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued purchase constitutes acceptance of the refund policy in effect at the time of purchase.
