What is ALSA?

ALSA is the sovereign utility token of the Alsania ecosystem. Built on Polygon for fast, low-cost transactions, ALSA powers staking rewards, governance, and gasless transactions through the Alsania Paymaster. It features a built-in anti-sweeper protocol that protects holders from unauthorized transfers.
